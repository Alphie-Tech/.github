## 🚀 Our Mission

At **Alphie Tech**, we aim to **empower creators, developers, and educational institutions** by delivering high-quality software, tools, and platforms that foster innovation and collaboration.

---

## 💡 What We Build

- **Custom Websites** – Tailored to meet unique client requirements.  
- **Web Applications** – Scalable full-stack solutions using modern technologies.  
- **Mobile Applications** – Cross-platform apps using Ionic, Cordova, and Expo.  
- **Web3 Applications** – Blockchain-integrated solutions.  
- **Community Platforms** – Systems for collaboration and engagement.  
- **E-commerce Websites** – Complete online shopping solutions.  
- **Developer Tools & Libraries** – Open-source projects to accelerate development.  

---

## 🛠 Technology Stack

- **Backend:** Laravel, PHP, Sanctum Authentication, Python FastAPI, NativePHP  
- **Frontend:** React, Inertia.js, TailwindCSS, Framer Motion, Lucide React, React Hot Toast  
- **Mobile & Hybrid:** Ionic, Cordova, Expo  
- **Version Control & Collaboration:** Git, GitHub
